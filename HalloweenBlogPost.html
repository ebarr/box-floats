Pumpkin Everything

This time of year, every brand thinks that they need to turn their product into a pumpkin. Not literally a pumpkin but add a pumpkin flavor to it. Some of these products include pumpkin bagels, pumpkin M&Ms, pumpkin beer and cider (which is actually amazing), Pumpkin vodka, pumpkin Pringles, pumpkin ice cream, Pumpkin Pop Tarts and Hershey Kisses.

I'll just stick to the Pumpkin cider though.

Excuse to Carve Pumpkins

Halloween gives me an excuse to make Ryan drive me to the suburbs, pick out a pumpkin, drag it up four flights of stairs and then throw it out five days later.

The only reason why I even like to get the pumpkin is to make pumpkin seeds. Without Halloween, I'd never be able to make pumpkin seeds.

Loads of Free Entertainment

Every year, you go to someone's house party, to the bars or both. This always provides a lot of entertainment and some awkward conversations.

Some costumes deserve awards while others shouldn't be legal to wear in public. Either way, it is always memorable.
